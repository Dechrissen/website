Solus Red, Green, and Blue

Released 11 September 2024

Pokémon Solus RGB is romhack of Red/Blue which aims to be a minimal, near-vanilla enhancement of the original game with added focus on the solo gameplay experience. Importantly, it doesn't take too many creative liberties or stray too far from the original design. To learn more, peruse the links below.
