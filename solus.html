1. Solus Red/Green/Blue

Pokémon Solus RGB is fan-made romhack of Red/Blue which aims to be a minimal, near-vanilla enhancement with a focus on the solo gameplay experience (by mitigating the need for trading). There are some other changes as well, so please peruse the links below.
