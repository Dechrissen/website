Welcome

My hope is that it also serves as a form of motivation for working on my projects, given that it'll help me to visualize my progress. To learn a little bit about me, check the about° tab. In the projects° tab, you will find an overview of my in-progress and completed projects. In the blog° tab, you will find more detailed descriptions of some of these projects, along with occasional entries on other topics. An archive of my academic papers can be found in the papers° tab.
