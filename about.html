About me

I graduated from Stony Brook University with a Master's in Computational Linguistics in January 2021. I'm interested in coding, gadgetry, and general tinkering. I also enjoy writing and I maintain a small blog on this website.

I'm also a minimalist (or essentialist, or intentionalist, or whatever you'd like to call it). I think it's important to consider the value that things I own provide, and I try not to make purchases that I don't truly want. I like to find ways to simplify certain aspects of my life. I see it as a journey toward mental clarity, so I can focus on things that matter to me.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, working on projects, and running.
